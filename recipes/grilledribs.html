Grilled Ribs Recipe

Smoky and Tender BBQ Ribs

Nothing beats the rich, smoky flavor of perfectly cooked BBQ ribs. Slow-cooked to tender perfection and coated in a savory, tangy sauce, these ribs are a backyard favorite. Whether grilled over an open flame or baked low and slow in the oven, they develop a deep caramelized crust that locks in their juicy goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 racks of pork ribs
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Make the spice rub.
  2. Trim the ribs, then season with the spice mix.
  3. Cook the ribs on the grill according to the detailed recipe below.
  4. Brush the grilled baby back ribs with barbecue sauce.
  5. Grill for five more minutes.
